Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Minced beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and tomato sauce.
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes.
