Pizza

Description

A classic homemade pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  2. Add mozzarella cheese.
  3. Add your toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven at 220°C for 12–15 minutes.
